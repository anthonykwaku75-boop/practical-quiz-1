Technology & Sustainability

Technology

Technology is the practical application of scientific knowledge to solve problems and improve human life. It supports communication, transportation, healthcare, education, and industrial development in modern society.

Environment

The environment consists of all living and non-living things that surround human beings. These include air, water, land, plants, animals, and natural resources. Human activities greatly affect the balance of the environment.

Innovation

Innovation involves creating new ideas, tools, or methods that improve efficiency and sustainability. Innovations help reduce environmental problems.
